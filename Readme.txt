ABOUT SKY WARRIOR:
Sky Warrior is a third person plane shooting game with 5 diferent levels, with different types of enemy planes with different types fires and different types of enemy bosses.

EXECUTION:
Execute "Sky Warrior.exe" to Play.

KEY HELP:
Move Mouse - Move Plane
Left Click - Fire
Right Click - Bomb
Escape - Pause
